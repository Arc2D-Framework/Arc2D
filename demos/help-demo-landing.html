What is WayPoint?

WayPoint is a powerful JavaScript library designed to make software learning a breeze. It acts as your virtual guide, leading users through step-by-step click paths to accomplish tasks that may seem challenging at first. With WayPoint, say goodbye to confusion and welcome a seamless learning experience.

Key Features:

  • Intuitive Guidance: Users are gently led through the software interface, ensuring a smooth learning curve.
  • Interactive Tutorials: Engaging tutorials with interactive elements to enhance understanding.
  • Task Completion Tracking: Monitor user progress and ensure they master every aspect of your software.
